# ADR-0001: Progressive Disclosure

## Status

accepted

## Context

We have an initial issue where this website will be trying to do two things at once. One is we want it to be good for recruiters and have it easy to read and digest, but also be good for documentation and engineering processes. 
A recruiter wants a very easily scannable website, whereas engineers will want that opposing depth.

## Decision

Content layering is the way to go. Keeping the surface clean and easy to digest, whereas the other content lives a click away. For recruiters this is important so they know what they are looking for as soon as the website open up.

## Alternatives Considered
We could have done all surface material, but it does not keep what I want with the depth. 

There was also the decision I could have made to make every part of the site a deep dive into things, but that would have also defeated the purpose of a portfolio.

## Consequences

The good thing is that recruiters will have the ability to skim the page without taking a long time to get what they need, and if the skimmer is more curious, they have the ability to get into the nitty-gritty.

Although with having depth be past surface level, if no one pushes through, then some of my best work is hidden behind a digital wall.
